## Project Overview & Objective
The Shopify Sales & Customer Analysis Dashboard is an end-to-end Power BI project designed to analyze e-commerce sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, retention metrics, and regional trends.
The objective of this project is to help business stakeholders track revenue growth, understand customer funnel behavior, identify high-performing regions and products, and optimize payment methods using data-driven insights.

This dashboard enables quick decision-making by presenting KPIs, trends, and breakdowns in a single interactive view.

## Problem Statement
E-commerce businesses often struggle with:
- Lack of visibility into overall sales performance
- Limited understanding of customer retention vs new customers
- Difficulty identifying top-performing regions, cities, and products
- No clear insight into preferred payment gateways
- Inability to track sales trends over time for forecasting

The goal was to build a centralized analytical dashboard that answers these questions clearly and visually.

## Data Visualization & Insights
### 1. Transaction Performance Analysis
KPIs Displayed:
- Net Sales: $4.18M
- Total Quantity Sold: 7,534
- Net Average Order Value: $562.6

#### Insights:
- Strong overall revenue generation with a high average order value
- Indicates effective pricing and product bundling strategies

### 2. Customer Purchase Behavior
KPIs Displayed:
- Total Customers: 4,431
- Single Order Customers: 2,392
- Repeat Customers: 2,039

#### Insights:
- Nearly 46% repeat customer rate, showing strong customer loyalty
- Significant opportunity to convert single-order customers into repeat buyers

### 3. Retention & Value Metrics
KPIs Displayed:
- Customer Lifetime Value (LTV): $943.6
- Repeat Rate: 46%
- Purchase Frequency: 1.68

#### Insights:
- Healthy lifetime value indicates strong long-term revenue potential
- Repeat rate suggests retention strategies are effective but can be further optimized

## 4.Net Sales Trend Over Time
Visuals Used:
- Line chart for net sales trend
- Bar chart for order volume distribution

#### Insights:
- Sales show seasonal fluctuations with clear peaks
- Helps identify high-demand periods for promotions and inventory planning

### 5. Regional Sales Analysis
Visuals Used:
- Map visualization (Province & City-wise)
- Top cities bar chart

#### Insights:
- Certain cities and provinces dominate sales performance
- Regional demand concentration helps in targeted marketing and logistics planning

### 6. Payment Gateway Analysis
Visual Used:
- Donut chart for payment method contribution

#### Insights:
- Shopify Payments contribute the highest share of net sales
- Alternative gateways like PayPal and Gift Cards still play a significant role
- Insights help optimize payment options for better conversion rates

### 7. Product Performance Analysis
Visual Used:
- Bar chart for net sales by product type

#### Insights:
- Few product categories generate the majority of revenue (Pareto principle)
- Low-performing products identified for improvement or discontinuation
- Helps prioritize inventory and marketing spend

## Conclusion
The Shopify Sales & Customer Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance by integrating sales, customer behavior, retention, regional, payment, and product insights into a single interactive Power BI solution.

Key Business Value:
- Enables data-driven decision-making
- Improves understanding of customer funnel & retention
- Identifies high-revenue regions, products, and gateways
- Supports sales forecasting and strategic planning

This dashboard can be effectively used by business owners, marketing teams, and sales analysts to optimize growth, improve customer experience, and maximize profitability.
